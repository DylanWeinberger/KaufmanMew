Our Personal Injury Settlements and Verdicts

The Law Offices of Mark E. Weinberger P.C. is a leading New York personal injury law firm and we have championed the cause of seriously injured New York accident victims for over 30 years. Since our founding, our personal injury attorneys have helped numerous clients and recovered millions of dollars in personal injury settlements and verdicts.

  • $2.2 Million Verdict Against MTA (NYC Transit Authority) – A 62 year old male was struck by a NYC bus and suffered multiple fractures to his left hip, left foot, left ankle and toes that led to a double hip replacement. The jury returned a verdict of full liability, finding the drivers negligence was a substantial factor in causing the accident.
  • $1,983,000 – For woman’s fatal infection caused by a car accident.
  • $1,570,000 – Scalding hot water in bath burned 1 year old infant that underwent reconstructive plastic surgery. Due to negligent landlord, building management and plumbing companies.
  • $1.5 Million – For tenant sexually assaulted in apartment building incinerator room – Post Traumatic Stress Disorder – Inadequate security in high crime area.
  • $1.5 Million – Wrongful death of motorcyclist hit by debris from passing truck.
  • $1.5 Million– Awarded to 7 year old child for lead paint poisoning. 2 years later family was awarded an additional $770,000 for a second incident, same child.

  • $1.25 Million – Construction accident leaves a 35 year old laborer with pelvic damage.

Social Host Laws applied. Injuries included fractured femur which required multiple surgeries causing scarring and loss of full range of motion.
